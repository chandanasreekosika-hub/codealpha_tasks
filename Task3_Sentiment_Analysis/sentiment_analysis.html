def get_sentiment(score):
    if score >= 4:
        return "Positive"
    elif score == 3:
        return "Neutral"
    else:
        return "Negative"

df['Sentiment'] = df['Score'].apply(get_sentiment)

    background_color='white'

Conclusion¶

  1. Most reviews are Positive, indicating high customer satisfaction.
  2. Ratings of 4 and 5 stars dominate the dataset.
  3. Positive words such as "Great", "Good", "Best", and "Love" appear frequently.
  4. Negative and Neutral reviews are significantly fewer than Positive reviews.
  5. The analysis shows that customers generally have a favorable opinion of the products.

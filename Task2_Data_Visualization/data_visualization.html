Superstore Data Visualization Project¶

Objective¶

Analyze Superstore sales data using various visualizations to identify trends, sales performance, regional distribution, and profit patterns.

Tools Used¶

  • Python
  • Pandas
  • Matplotlib
  • Seaborn

Visualizations Created¶

  1. Sales by Category (Bar Chart)
  2. Sales by Region (Pie Chart)
  3. Profit by Category
  4. Top 10 Subcategories
  5. Sales vs Profit (Scatter Plot)
  6. Correlation Heatmap

Key Insights¶

  • Technology category generated the highest sales.
  • West region contributed the largest share of sales.
  • Some high-sales orders resulted in losses.
  • Discounts negatively impacted profit in several cases.
  • Certain subcategories significantly outperformed others.
